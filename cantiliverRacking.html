Cantilever Racking

Efficient Storage for Oversized Items

Cantilever Racking is a specialized storage solution designed to accommodate long, bulky, or irregularly shaped items that don't fit well on traditional shelving or pallet racking systems. It offers a space-efficient and accessible way to store items like pipes, lumber, steel bars, sheet materials, and more. Cantilever Racking is the preferred choice for businesses that deal with oversized products and need to maintain organization and accessibility.

Each of these storage solutions is tailored to address specific storage challenges and requirements, ensuring that your warehouse or facility operates efficiently and effectively. Our team can work with you to assess your needs and recommend the most suitable solution for your business. Contact us today to discuss how our services can enhance your storage and logistics operations.

Key Features

Open Design: Cantilever Racking features horizontal arms extending from vertical columns, creating an open and unobstructed storage system. This open design allows for easy loading and unloading of items with varying lengths.

Adjustable Arms: The arms on Cantilever Racking are adjustable, allowing you to customize the system to fit the specific dimensions of your products. This adaptability ensures efficient use of space.

High Load Capacity: Cantilever Racking is constructed from heavy-duty steel, providing exceptional strength and load-bearing capacity. It is capable of supporting large and heavy items.

Accessibility: Despite its robust design, Cantilever Racking offers easy access to stored items. This accessibility is crucial for businesses that require quick retrieval of oversized materials.

Versatility: These racks are versatile and suitable for various industries, including construction, manufacturing, retail, and warehouses. They can be used to store a wide range of long and bulky items.

Applications



Construction Sites: Cantilever Racking is commonly used on construction sites to store and organize materials like lumber, pipes, and steel beams.

Manufacturing Facilities: Manufacturers utilize Cantilever Racking to store raw materials, finished products, and parts with irregular shapes.

Retail Outlets: Retailers use Cantilever Racking in their backrooms to store and manage oversized merchandise like furniture, appliances, and building materials.

Warehouses:Warehouses dealing with products such as carpets, rolls of fabric, and heavy machinery benefit from Cantilever Racking for efficient storage.

Customization: Cantilever Racking systems can be customized to accommodate different item lengths, weights, and load capacities. You can adjust the number of arms and their spacing to fit your specific requirements

Safety Considerations

Safety is essential when using Cantilever Racking. Proper training for forklift operators, regular inspections, and load capacity adherence are crucial for maintaining a safe working environment.

Conclusion

Cantilever Racking is a space-efficient and accessible solution for businesses dealing with oversized and irregularly shaped items. Its ability to
maximize storage space while maintaining easy access to stored materials makes it a valuable asset in various industries. Whether you're a
construction company, manufacturer, retailer, or warehouse, Cantilever Racking can help you optimize your storage space and improve overall
efficiency. Contact us today to explore how Cantilever Racking can transform your storage capabilities and contribute to your business's success.
